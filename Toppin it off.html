Toppin' it Off

Toppin' it Off is the mother/daughter team of Venessa and Lydia Laman, the best bakers in Grand Prairie/Mansfield!

We bake custom cakes, cupcakes, cookies, macarons, and are always adding new and exciting flavors to our lineup! We also hold child and adult decorating classes, so be sure to stay tuned for schedule updates!
